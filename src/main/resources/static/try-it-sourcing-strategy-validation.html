Sourcing strategy validation

SS01 - Affiliation

Rule(s):

  1. Role Type (role-seat-type-cd): = Geo
    AND
  2. Preferred Resource Channel (pref-resource-channel): = Affiliate

SS02 - CIC Domestic Center

Rule(s):

  1. Role Type (role-seat-type-cd): = GIC
    AND
  2. GIC Provider Country (gic-provider-country): in <list of CIC countries e.g., Canada, United States, Germany, Denmark, or to put it another way, the GIC Provider countries from Source tab of the template, row 1 where there is a GIC Delivery Organization called GBS: IBM Services Center in a row under that country heading> a.k.a, CIC Domestic Delivery Center
    AND
  3. Contract owning country (contract-owning-country) must be within the same Market as the GIC Provider Country (gic-provider-country).

SS03 - CIC Domestic Center - Contractor

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Domestic Center rule: (SS02)
    AND
  2. Request contractor / Accept Global Contractors (GIC) (req-cntrct-accept-gbl-delivery): = Yes<

SS04 - CIC Domestic Center - EPH

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Domestic Center rule: (SS02)
    AND
  2. Requested Band High (band-high): <= 6
    AND
  3. Requested Band Low (band-low): >= 6G

SS05 - CIC Global

Rule(s):

  1. Role Type (role-seat-type-cd): = GIC
    AND
  2. GIC Provider Country (gic-provider-country): in <list of GIC countries, e.g., India, China, Brazil, Romania, etc. or to put it another way GIC Provider countries from the Source tab of the template, row 1, where there is at least one GIC Delivery Organization that starts with GBS but is NOT called GBS: IBM Services Center in a row under that country heading> a.k.a, CIC Global Delivery Center

SS06 - CIC Domestic Center / Affiliate-on-client-site

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Domesttic Center rule: (SS02)
    OR
  2. Affiliate rule: (SS01)

SS07 - CIC Global / Affiliate

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Global Center rule: (SS05)
    OR
  2. Affiliate rule: (SS01)

SS08 - CIC Global OR CIC Domestic Center

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Global Center rule: (SS05)
    OR
  2. CIC Domestic Center rule: (SS02)

SS09 - CIC Global OR Contractor (Domestic)

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Global Center rule: (SS05)
    OR
  2. Contractor (Domestic) rule: (SS13)

SS10 - CIC Global OR Geo Regulars (Domestic)

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Global Center rule: (SS05)
    OR
  2. Geo Regulars (Domestic) rule: (SS14)

SS11 - CIC Global OR Geo Regulars (Domestic) B8+ / Affiliate <= B7

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Global Center rule: (SS05)
    OR
  2. (
    (Geo Regulars (Domestic) rule: (SS14)
    AND
    Requested Band High (band-high): >= 8)
    OR
  3. ( CIC Domestic Center rule: (SS02)
    AND
    Requested Band Low (band-low): <= 7)
    )

SS12 - CIC Global OR Geo Regulars (Domestic) B9+ / Affiliate <= B8

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Global Center rule: (SS05)
    OR
  2. (
    (Geo Regulars (Domestic) rule: (SS14)
    AND
    Requested Band High (band-high): >= 9)
    OR
  3. ( CIC Domestic Center rule: (SS02)
    AND
    Requested Band Low (band-low): <= 8)
    )

SS13 - Contractor (Domestic)

Rule(s):

  1. Role Type (role-seat-type-cd): = Geo
    AND
  2. Preferred Resource Channel (pref-resource-channel): = Contractor
    AND
  3. Request contractor / Accept Global Contractors (GIC) (req-cntrct-accept-gbl-delivery): = Yes

SS14 - Geo Regulars (Domestic)

Rule(s):

  1. Role Type (role-seat-type-cd): = Geo
    AND
  2. Preferred Resource Channel (pref-resource-channel): = Regular
    AND
  3. Request contractor / Accept Global Contractors (GIC) (req-cntrct-accept-gbl-delivery): = No

SS15 - Geo Regulars (Domestic) - EPH

Rule(s):

  1. Geo Regulars (Domestic) rule: (SS14)
    AND
  2. Requested Band High (band-high): <= 6
    AND
  3. Requested Band Low (band-low): >= 6G

SS16 - Geo Regulars (Domestic) B8+ / CIC Domestic Center <= B7

Rule(s):

  1. (Geo Regulars (Domestic) rule: (SS14)
    AND
  2. Requested Band High (band-high): >= 8)
    OR
  3. (CIC Domestic Center rule: (SS02)
    AND
  4. Requested Band Low (band-low): <= 7)

SS17 - Geo Regulars (Domestic) B8+ / CIC Domestic Center <= B7

Rule(s):

  1. (Geo Regulars (Domestic) rule: (SS14)
    AND
  2. Requested Band High (band-high): >= 8)
    OR
  3. (CIC Global rule: (SS05)
    AND
  4. Requested Band Low (band-low): <= 7)

SS18 - Indirect

Rule(s):

  1. Always set to "Unable tto determine"

SS19 - Other

Rule(s):

  1. Always set to "Unable tto determine"

SS20 - CIC Global OR CIC Domestic Center / Affiliate-on-client-site

Rule(s):

  1. CIC Global Center rule: (SS05)
    OR
  2. CIC Domesttic Center rule: (SS02)
    OR
  3. Affiliate rule: (SS01)
